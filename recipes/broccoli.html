Broccoli

Description

Roasted broccoli is easy to make and so much more flavorsome than boiled. My favorite part is the roasted sliced stem pieces.

Ingredients

  • 14 ounces broccoli
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients and preheat oven
  2. Cut broccoli florets from the stalk
  3. Peel the stalk and slice
  4. Mix florets and stem pieces with olive oil
  5. Roast in oven until tender and lightly browned
